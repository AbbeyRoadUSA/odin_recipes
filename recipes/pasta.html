Garlic Butter Pasta

A simple dish made with only 4 ingredients

This may seem like a boring dish but don't judge a book by its cover. This pasta dish is bright, rich and creamy.


Ingredients

  • All Purpose Flour - 1/4 Cup
  • Unsweeted Cocoa Powder - 2 TBSPs
  • Granulated Sugar - 3 TBSPs
  • Baking Powder - 1/4 TSP
  • Salt - Pinch
  • Milk - 1/4 Cup
  • Oil (Canola, vegetable, etc) - 2 TBSPs
  • Vanilla Extract - 1/4 TSP
  • Semi-sweet Chocolate Chips - 1 TBSP
