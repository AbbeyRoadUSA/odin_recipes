Garlic Butter Pasta

A simple dish made with only 4 ingredients

This may seem like a boring dish but don't judge a book by its cover. This pasta dish is bright, rich and creamy.


Ingredients

  • Pasta - 3oz dry
  • Salted Butter - 2 TBSPs
  • Garlic - 2 Cloves
  • Lemon - 1/2 Juiced
  • Reserved Pasta Water - 1/4 Cup

Steps

  1. Fill up a medium sauce-pan with water.
  2. Heavily salt the water and bring to a boil.
  3. Prepare the dried pasta per the package instructions.
  4. Mince the two cloves of garlic and juice half the lemon. Set aside.
  5. In a non-stick skillet, melt the butter on low heat.
  6. Add the minced garlic to the melted butter.
  7. Once pasta is al dente. Reserve 1/4 cup of pasta water. Then drain pasta.
  8. Add pasta to the skillet and mix.
  9. Add 1 TBSP of pasta water to skillet. Mix well to create a silky sauce.
  10. Plate the pasta. Add a bit of lemon juice before serving. Enjoy!
