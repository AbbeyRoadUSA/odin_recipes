Chocolate Mug Cake

A great easy dessert for one!

When that chocolate craving hits, this recipe is here to save the day. Everyone knows when you have a craving you don't have the patience to bake an entire cake. That's just one of the reasons this recipe shines!


Ingredients

  • All Purpose Flour - 1/4 Cup
  • Unsweeted Cocoa Powder - 2 TBSPs
  • Granulated Sugar - 3 TBSPs
  • Baking Powder - 1/4 TSP
  • Salt - Pinch
  • Milk - 1/4 Cup
  • Oil (Canola, vegetable, etc) - 2 TBSPs
  • Vanilla Extract - 1/4 TSP
  • Semi-sweet Chocolate Chips - 1 TBSP

Steps

  1. Grease a 12oz mug with nonstick spray.
  2. In mug, mix together flour, unsweetened cocoa powder, granulated sugar, baking powder and salt.
  3. Add milk, oil and vanilla extract to mug. Mix will to combine.
  4. Sprinkle chocolate chips on top.
  5. Place mug in microwave and microwave for 60-90 seconds. Until the center of cake is just set.
  6. Carefully remove mug from microwave and allow to cool before enjoying!
